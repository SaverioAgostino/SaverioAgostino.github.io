Virgin Cucumber Gimlet

Description:

A classic drink with a fresh, non-alcoholic twist - irresistible & easy to make mocktail!


Ingredients:

  • 1.5 oz club soda
  • 4-5 slices of muddled cucumber
  • 1 oz fresh lime juice
  • 1 oz simple syrup

Steps:

  1. Combine all ingredients and shake with ice
  2. Serve in a rocks glass over crushed ice
  3. Garnish with a rolled cucumber slice
